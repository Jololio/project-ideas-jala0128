CAPSTONE MVP 2:

Pitch: An app that behaves like Amazon, or any other online retailer where a user can shop for products and add them to a cart

MVP:

Users can search for items in the store

Users can click on the item and read a short description of the item, and it's relevant information (Price, quantity, etc)

Users can add items to their cart

Users can view their cart and proceed to a checkout


Future Features:

Users can login to their own account

Users can create their own account
