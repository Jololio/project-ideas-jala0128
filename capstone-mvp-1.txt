CAPSTONE MVP 1:

Pitch: An app that lets you search up restaurants nearby and add them to a list that will auto pick one from that last and show you
all the information (Directions, address, phone, etc)


MVP:

Users can search for nearby restaurants

Users can add restaurants to their own list of restaurants to choose from

Users can click a button to auto select a restaurant from their list of restaurants

Users will be able to see the restaurants information (Directions, address, phone number, etc)

Users can, once a restaurant has been selected, click on a button to show directions to the restaurant


Future Features:

Users can create their own restaurant lists and can select a list to randomly select from

Users can add restaurants to different restaurant lists that they've created
